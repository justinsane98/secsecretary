            • Potential Technical Changes to EDGAR Filer Access and Filer Account Management Processes

              The Securities and Exchange Commission (``Commission&#39;&#39;) is requesting comment on potential technical changes related to how entities and individuals access the Commission&#39;s Electronic Data Gathering, Analysis, and Retrieval system (``EDGAR&#39;&#39;) to make submissions, and how these entities and individuals (``filers&#39;&#39;) manage the permissions of individuals who may file on EDGAR on their behalf. Individuals who seek to file on EDGAR on behalf of a filer would be directed to a third-party service provider to create individual user account credentials and to enable multifactor authentication. Each filer would designate a ``filer administrator&#39;&#39;--analogous to the current filer contact person who receives the filer&#39;s EDGAR access codes--to manage the permissions of the filer&#39;s individual users through a new filer management tool on EDGAR.
